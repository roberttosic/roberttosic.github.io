Info





This is the boxing simulator that I created using Unity Game Engine, and writting my scripts in C#. It allows players with working VR equipment to try boxing in a virtual environment in either a single player mode where the user can hit the punching bag for as long as they wish, or a multiplayer mode where they may connect to another user over a local network and see who could come out the winner! If you want to take a look at the source files, you can click here to take a look.
