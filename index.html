About Me

I am a computer science major that has recently graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor of Science (honours). Having worked on numerous projects throughout many years, it is safe to say that this field of work has become a passion of mine. Being able to look at a problem in front of me, and being able to solve it using the logic, knowledge, and skills that I have gained throughout my years of study and development is something I deeply enjoy. I always try to keep up with new ideas in technology, as things never stop changing. As a result of this, I have studied theory on artificial intelligence, created my own virtual reality app, put work into web development and the different frameworks there are to use, and more.

I am able to work in many different programming languages, such as C/C++/C#, SQL, Java, Python , Rust, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript/Perl/Django/PHP. I have worked with many different operating systems throughout the last four or five years, such as Windows (7/8/10), macOS (Sierra/El Capitan/Yosemite), as well as Linux (Ubuntu/Red Hat/Deepin). I have had my hand in game development using both Unity and OpenGL primarily. I have worked on web development extensively, doing both front end and back end work. I have deep understanding of the theory behind cryptography, having created a light version of DES, and I have studied computer networks extensively, creating my own peer to peer client file, as well as a file distribution network demonstration.
